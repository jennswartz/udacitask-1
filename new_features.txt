Feature #1:  Adds priority status to the To Do list.  The two options are: "Do Now" or 
"Procrastinate."  The priority status is initially set to "Do Now" but can by changed.  "Do
Now" is the false boolean, and procrastinate is the true.

Feature #2:  Deletes completed items from the list.  

Feature #3:  Prints the list to a text file.
